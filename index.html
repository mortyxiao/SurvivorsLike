The Dark Tale

🏰

In the cursed Italian countryside of 2021, the evil Bisconte Draculó has unleashed hordes of supernatural monsters upon the land. As brave survivors from the Belpaese family, you must venture through haunted libraries, cursed forests, and otherworldly chapels to stop this ancient evil.

Ironically, despite its vampire-themed name, you won't encounter a single vampire in your journey - instead, prepare to face an army of nightmarish creatures in this Gothic horror masterpiece that has captivated millions worldwide.
