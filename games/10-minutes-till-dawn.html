Game Information

10 Minutes Till Dawn is an intense bullet-hell survival game inspired by Vampire Survivors. Fight endless waves of monsters, collect power-ups, and survive until dawn breaks. Best experienced on desktop with keyboard and mouse.

Survival Tips

🎯

Focus on Movement

Keep moving constantly! Standing still is certain death. Learn to navigate through bullet patterns while dealing damage.

⚡

Prioritize Upgrades

Choose upgrades that complement each other. Area damage and projectile speed are often more valuable than raw damage.

🧠

Learn Enemy Patterns

Each enemy type has unique behavior. Understanding their movement and attack patterns is key to survival.

💎

Collect Experience

Don't ignore experience gems! They're crucial for leveling up and gaining new abilities to survive longer.
